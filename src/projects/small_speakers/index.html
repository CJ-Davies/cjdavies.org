3" Tang Band speakers

2012

Home

This was my first time designing & simulating a mid/high speaker entirely from scratch in WinISD & was only partly successful.

I had a lot of help from some far more knowledgeable builders on the diyAudio forums to attempt to salvage the design, but they never really sounded very good. The positive outcome was that one of the pairs of 3" Tang Band drivers you see in these photos would eventually make it into my first pair of TABAQ TL speakers.

Features

  • Tang Band W3-926SD & W3-871C drivers
  • Folded shelf port
