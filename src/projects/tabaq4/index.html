My fourth (!) pair of TABAQ speakers.

I scaled up Bjørn's original plans to accommodate the thicker 18mm material. You can download these modified plans here (PDF).

I was especially pleased at how well the 3D printed jigs I designed to route the driver rebates worked. I've certainly improved since my very first pair of TABAQ speakers back in 2018!

The PLS-P830987 drivers really do sound fantastic in these boxes. I run them with no baffle step correction, just a very slight reduction in treble via the tone controls on the AIYIMA A07 Pro (TPA3255) amplifier I use to drive them.

For more about the TABAQ designs, see Bjørn's LinkedIn post on the 20th anniversary of the design, or peruse all 20 years of community discussion at the original diyAudio forum thread.

Features

  • Reclaimed 1970s hardwood ply
  • Dowel joints throughout
  • PLS-P830987 drivers
  • Drivers rebated front & back
