As seen front row in the snow at Anjunadeep Open Air Red Rocks 2024!

WS2812 'NeoPixel' RGB LEDs & white 3D printed PLA for a pretty version of the logo from Above & Beyond's Anjunabeats/Anjunadeep record labels.

First iteration used an Adafruit QT Py SAMD21 with effects from the Adafruit Neopixel library, second iteration used an Adafruit QT Py ESP32 Pico with WiFi control courtesy of WLED.

This project was the starting point for what would eventually become my AnjunaGlow & AnjunaGlow Mini projects, which use custom PCBs & add audio reactive effects via a built in microphone.

STL files available on Printables - v1, v2 & v3.

Features

  • 3D printed enclosure
  • Standard self-adhesive WS2812 LED tape
  • Sideways mounted LEDs to aid diffusion
