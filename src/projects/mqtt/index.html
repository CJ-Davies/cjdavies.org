My first foray into home automation & IoT with MQTT.

Using cheap Wemos/LOLIN D1 Mini ESP8266 MCU boards & even cheaper DHT22 temperature/humidity sensors to report environmental data to Adafruit IO.

STL files available on Thingiverse, but be aware that the lack of thermal separation between the sensors & the MCU boards skews the readings. My later design vastly improved upon this original concept.

Features

  • Very cheap per unit (< 5USD)
  • Friction fit 3D printed enclosures
