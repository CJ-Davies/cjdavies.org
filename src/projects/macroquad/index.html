A fully custom carbon design that uses square hardwood dowel or aluminium box section for arms

After my Everycopter project I wanted to create a quad frame with far more build space in the centre for multiple electronics stacks & room for multiple battery packs on top, but which still used the same approach to mounting arms made from hardware store materials like hardwood dowel or aluminium box section.

My favourite feature of this build is the dual XT60E1-M connectors that allow for easy connection of two battery packs in parallel.

An accompanying series of build videos is available on YouTube.

Features

  • Numerous 20x20/30.5x30.5 mounting positions
  • Pixhawk Cube mounting position
  • Dual XT60E1-M support for parallel battery connections
  • Top & bottom plates support easy payload attachment via 1/4" tripod bolts
