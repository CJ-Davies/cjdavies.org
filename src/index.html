Hello, I'm CJ!

Passionate about creating things that (usually) work. My projects span PCB design, IoT, 3D printing, HiFi audio & drones. I hold a PhD in Computer Science & have worked commercially with drones in digital heritage & healthcare. I embrace a design-make-iterate philosophy, sharing my work online as I go. Feel free to explore my projects, drop a comment/DM on the relevant platforms, or mail me if you have any questions!
